THESIS
Although STEAM education has received a lot of endorsement from scholars and teachers, it is still a relatively new area that lacks clarity in what students learn. Thus, the current research is interested in analyzing the learning outcomes and perspective shifts reflected through studentsâ€™ creation process and end-products. Beyond integrating an art activity with photography, the current study embeds an open-ended curriculum design challenge and a peer testing session. Designing a curriculum requires high-level understanding of the design thinking and computational thinking, and through testing curriculum, students can give critical feedback and gain greater understanding of how the peers think.
